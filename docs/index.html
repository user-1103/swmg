Small World Mini-Games

Picture this: you are at a rat themed amusement park and some member of your party is hell bent on riding a musical shrine to creepy dolls across the world. For the wayward souls who find themselves in this situation I offer you two games to maintain your sanity.

Small World Paparazzi

Each member of your party is a paparazzi assigned a unique set of targets to capture on film before the ride is over.

Small World Assassins

Someone has had enough of this forsaken ride and its creepy dolls! They have put a hit out on a particular set of targets and the first member of your party to take them out gets paid.
