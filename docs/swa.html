Small World Assassins

  1. Before you ride, one member of your party takes a screenshot of this page and sends it to a shared chat.
  2. While you ride each member of your party is trying to take a photo of the listed targets.
  3. The first person to take a photo of the target and post it to the shared chat gets the associated points.
  4. The person with the most points wins!
